Mission

Yugantar — meaning "a new era" or "a turning point" — is not a channel about one thing. It is a platform born from the refusal to be boxed into a niche. From the corridors of power in global capitals to the timeless questions of consciousness and existence, Yugantar explores it all.

Rooted in Bharatiya thought and driven by global curiosity, Yugantar is a space where geopolitics meets philosophy, where whistleblowing meets strategy, where science meets soul. The lion and the sun are not just symbols — they are a declaration: power tempered by wisdom, awakening fuelled by knowledge.
